Vibration measurements:

Vibrations are a repetitive - rigid or flexible - motion of (solid) parts. Vibration measurements require motion sensors to measure acceleration, velocity and – less often – position at the point(s) of interest. Depending on the needs, additional (pressure, flow, flux, intensity, …) sensors to characterise the excitation source might also be needed.
